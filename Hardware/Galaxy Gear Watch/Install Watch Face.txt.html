You can install Galaxy Gear watch faces from the Galaxy Store or by using the Galaxy Wearable app [Galaxy Store] - Click on Menu, option My Apps - Click on the tab Watch - Find watch face - Click button Download [Galaxy Wearable app] - [Bottom bar] - Click link Discover -> Navigates page - [Top Watch Faces card] - Click link View More -> Navigates page - Login to Samsung Store - Click on Menu, option My Apps - Click on option All - Find watch face - Click button Download
