Get a Google Plus-code for your area [Chrome browser] - [https://plus.codes/map/] - Click three-lines menu, option Satellite - Click three-lines menu, option Grid - [Navigate to location] - Zoom in and click a single Plus Code rectangle - Copy the browser address bar - Note: If you are in Chrome non-incognito mode, you can create a desktop shortcut to the current website page - Note: The plus codes are 8 characters, a hyphen, and two or three suffix characters. The bottom of the map only shows you the two suffix characters. The three suffix characters are in the address bar. [Email app] - Paste the address into an email
