The Auto-generated English caption file is viewable from the standard YouTube interface [YouTube.com] - [Below the video window, to the right of the Share and Save buttons] - Click the three-dots menu, select Open Transcript -> Opens DIV to right of video - Use Ctrl-A then Ctrl-C to copy all text to the clipboard [Notepad++] - Paste all text into a new text document - Search for "00:" to find the first line of the captions - Search for "Up next" to find the end of the captions
