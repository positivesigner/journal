Make a copy of the same survey to send to a different user.

  • Click the form's File menu

  • Click the option "Make a copy"

  • Enter a new survey name

  • Click the OK button to open a new window for the copied survey

Continued from 5) Copy Survey

Use the Responses tab to link the new survey copy to another tab in the same spreadsheet as the first survey.

  • Click the Responses tab

  • Click the Response options menu

  • Click the option "Select response destination"

  • Click the option "Select existing spreadsheet" and click the Select button

  • Click the name of the previous spreadsheet

  • Click the spreadsheet icon to see the response records

  • You can see the responses from the first survey by clicking on the other spreadsheet tab. You may rename the spreadsheet tabs.
